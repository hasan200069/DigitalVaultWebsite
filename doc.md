AI-powered Digital Storage Vault —
Features & Milestones Breakdown
1. Introduction
Purpose: This document outlines the features, milestones, and estimated effort for building
the AI-powered hybrid digital storage vault.
Scope Summary: The vault provides secure storage for sensitive digital assets (crypto/NFT
keys, wills, property documents, passwords, insurance) with bank-grade encryption,
inheritance protocol, AI-powered categorization, and white-label customization.
Assumptions:
● Development team size: 3–4 members
● Timeline: Phased milestones.
